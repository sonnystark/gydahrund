My Services

Learn Icelandic

I offer wide variety of different niveau levels of Icelandic courses, both group courses and private lessons that can be tailor made to suit your level and needs. With over a decade of teaching experience, I combine language learning with culture and history of Iceland into a comprehensive course with focus on speaking fluency and colloquial usage of the language without over-complicating the process. No previous Icelandic or language learning experience necessary. Courses are available in English, svenska and Deutsch.
